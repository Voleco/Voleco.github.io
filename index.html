About Me

I'm a passionate AI researcher and engineer with a deep interest in advancing intelligent systems.

During my PhD, I specialized in heuristic search algorithms, exploring efficient methods for solving complex optimization problems in AI planning and decision-making.

At PuduRobotics, I led the development of the company's second-generation Multi-Agent Path Finding (MAPF) project, optimizing navigation and coordination for multi-robot systems in real-world environments. Additionally, I spearheaded a groundbreaking initiative to build an intelligent voice agent from scratch, leveraging Large Language Models (LLMs) to enable natural, context-aware interactions.
